About CryptoLottery

Cryptolottery is a decentralized lottery platform that is Ethereum-based with no human involvement. This platform offers different types of games. Lottery game is currently functional and rest other games are under construction. The platform is a crypto-exchange that allows players to buy their tickets in cryptocurrencies like Ether.
